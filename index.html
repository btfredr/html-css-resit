Conscious Coffee

Conscious Coffee is a new chain of establishment aiming to give our customers the best coffee experience possible. Our main vision is to provide customers with fresh ingredients that are environmentally friendly.

Just as some love languages or beautiful landscapes, others have a great passion for coffee. We like to think that everyone can be influenced by the attractiveness of prayer, so we open the coffee master program to anyone who becomes a barista (also called a partner) at Conscious Coffee, even though they can't distinguish crema from cappuccino.

From day one, we show our partners what it is all about, and they are introduced to an alluring world of taste and texture. We take them on a journey to the various cultivation areas around the world and help them develop a real connoisseur palate from the start. Not only do they gain basic knowledge of coffee and the rich history of coffee, they also learn how to make their own coffee and show this knowledge to you in the store.
